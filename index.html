How To Play
From Login to War Council
in 3 Minutes
01
CHOOSE YOUR NATION
Pick from 10 nations — India, USA, China, Russia, UK, France, Pakistan, Türkiye, Israel, Japan. Each has unique military branches, roles, and geopolitical context loaded in.
02
SELECT YOUR CABINET ROLE
Are you the Prime Minister? Defence Minister? Army Chief? Naval Chief? NSA? Each role has different powers. PM chairs the NSC and calls votes. Army Chief commands ground ops.
03
INVITE YOUR TEAM
Share the room code. Up to 7 players per nation, 14 total in Nation vs Nation mode. Each teammate joins and claims a role. PM approves them into the war council.
04
CONVENE THE NSC
Situation room goes live — real-time voice with video tiles for every minister. Intelligence feeds populate. Threat alerts appear. The PM calls the first NSC meeting.
05
DECIDE. VOTE. EXECUTE.
Ministers debate strategy. PM calls a vote. The winning option — naval deterrence, precision airstrike, covert ops, economic sanctions — triggers a live combat simulation everyone watches together.
06
FULL DEBRIEF
After the operation: scored debrief, lessons learned, timeline replay, next strategic options. The conflict continues — new intelligence arrives, new decisions await.
